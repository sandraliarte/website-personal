Wording / Exercise

"You have to design a laboratory of architecture in a junction of 4 roads. The volume of air occupies a 25m radius, whose center is the crossroads. This area is open only in summer, being a public room. Requeriments: community workshop, 6 research rooms, 10 offices, a library, a conference room, toilets, access and transit spaces."

4 Ways + Ribbon

The solution is based on the way I understand a crossroads. The road should not force you to have to go, just guide, suggests an end, a route. Therefore, you can leave the way and watch around you, seeing people, looking at the place where you are. So, my project is a ribbon that guide with the way.

Inside=Outside

Thus, I thought that it is not appropriate to design a compact building, where you know "this space is inside" and "this space is outside". I thought the place wanted a space in which, when you were outside you were inside too. A space where the social life is so intensive. A project that is the way, and vice versa.

Published in the journal "Memory of Project, University of Zaragoza 2011-2012"
