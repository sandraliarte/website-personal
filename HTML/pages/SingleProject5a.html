Solution

The project was created by two ideas: conception of the individual who will occupy and the place as space.
+ Individual: large study halls do not allow study (you can see people come and go, sounds close, people in front of you...) thus the project is based on individuality within the community. As in the first diagram above: a person, a person and his table, a group of people forming a cell, a cell study... a set of cells creating the library.
+ Space (aristotle conception): the place exists only when is filling with which fill the place, that's the reason that every cell is illuminated if it is occupied or not. In the evening, light building will depend on the occupied cells.

Social Individuality

The notion of limit and meaning of neighborhood.

You can study in your cell and see others studying in other cells, without limiting visual and without discomfort. You are together, individuality together.

Density Degraded

Stratification of noise observed on each floor, above there is more noise and so on.
Also street floor is more public (quick lecture), and next levels (cells) for study.
Vertical connections are made with 8 vertical communication, which are the structure of cells and carry building systems.
