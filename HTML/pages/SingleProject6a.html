Wording / Exercise

The word Tensegrity comes from tensional integrity, a term coined by Fuller, which expose their definition: "Tensegrity is a structural system composed of discontinuous compression elements connected by continuous tension cables"

The work involves the design of a tensegrity structure with a calculation program (I-deas, software for finite element), in order to know if the design is a good tensegrity practice.
